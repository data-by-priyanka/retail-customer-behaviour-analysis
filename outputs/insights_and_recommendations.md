# Retail Customer Behaviour Analysis
## Insights & Recommendations

**Dataset:** 3,900 customers | **Revenue:** $233,081 | **AOV:** $59.76  
**Tools:** Python | MySQL | Power BI | Excel

---

## INSIGHT 1 — Clothing Dominates But Creates Concentration Risk

**Finding:** Clothing = $104,264 (44.7%). Clothing + Accessories = 76.5% of revenue.

**So What:** If Clothing demand drops, 44.7% of revenue is immediately at risk.

**Action:** Cross-sell Accessories to 1,737 Clothing buyers via email campaigns.

**Estimated Impact:** 1,737 × 20% × $59.76 = +$10,380 incremental revenue.

---

## INSIGHT 2 — Discounts Are Hurting Revenue, Not Helping

**Finding:** Discounted orders avg $59.28 vs $60.13 without. 
1,677 discounted orders × $0.85 = $1,425 margin erosion.

**So What:** Blanket discounts are reducing profit with no spend increase.

**Action:** Pause blanket discounts. Run A/B test with free shipping threshold instead.

**Estimated Impact:** Recover $1,425 margin immediately.

---

## INSIGHT 3 — 51+ Age Group Is Most Valuable, Most Ignored

**Finding:** 51+ = $88,480 (38% of revenue). Most retail marketing targets 18-35.

**So What:** Highest value demographic is completely underserved.

**Action:** Create a 51+ premium loyalty program with tailored communication.

**Estimated Impact:** 10% retention boost = +$8,848 protected revenue annually.

---

## INSIGHT 4 — Only 2.1% New Customers — Zero Growth Pipeline

**Finding:** 91.4% loyal, only 83 new customers out of 3,900.

**So What:** Business is 100% dependent on existing customers. Zero acquisition.

**Action:** Launch referral program using 3,563 loyal customers as brand ambassadors.

**Estimated Impact:** 50 new customers × $59.76 × 5 orders = +$14,940 LTV.

---

## INSIGHT 5 — 2,583 Loyal Customers Not Subscribed

**Finding:** Subscribers spend LESS ($59.49 vs $59.87). 
72.5% of loyal customers are not subscribed.

**So What:** Subscription messaging is broken — selling savings that don't exist.

**Action:** Reframe subscription as convenience not savings. Target 2,583 loyal non-subscribers.

**Estimated Impact:** 312 new subscribers × $59.49 = +$18,561 predictable recurring revenue.

---
